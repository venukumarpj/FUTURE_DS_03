# FUTURE_DS_03

# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

## Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of marketing funnel 
performance and customer conversion metrics derived from a bank marketing campaign 
dataset. The report is structured across three interactive pages, each focusing on 
a distinct analytical dimension.

**Page 1 – Customer Profile & Subscription Overview** presents key KPIs including 
Total Accounts, Average Age, Subscribed Count, and Average Balance. It enables 
demographic segmentation through slicers for Age Group, Marital Status, and Contact 
type, with visuals covering Subscription Rate by Job, Education, Housing, and Month.

**Page 2 – Campaign Performance** dives into outreach effectiveness, featuring a 
funnel chart for Campaign by Job, a line chart for Campaign Duration trends, and 
clustered charts breaking down campaign activity by Age Group and Contact channel — 
all filterable by Month and Marital Status.

**Page 3 – Economic & Temporal Indicators** explores macroeconomic influences on 
conversions, including Employee Variation Rate by Days, Education-wise Day Number 
distribution, Consumer Price Index analysis, weekly duration breakdowns, and Balance 
trends by Month.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Power BI Desktop

## Purpose
Designed to support data-driven marketing decisions by identifying high-converting 
customer segments and optimizing future campaign strategies.
